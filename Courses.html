Courses for Studying Abroad

At E A Overseas, we offer comprehensive guidance for various courses and examinations required for studying abroad. Below are some general courses and exams that we assist with:

General Courses

  • Bachelor's Degree Programs - Guidance on selecting universities and courses tailored to your career goals.
  • Master's Degree Programs - Support in choosing specialized programs and universities for postgraduate studies.
  • Diploma Courses - Information on short-term diploma programs in various fields.
  • Language Courses - Assistance with language proficiency courses (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL) necessary for admission.

Exams for Abroad Consultancy

  • IELTS (International English Language Testing System) - Preparation courses and resources for the IELTS exam.
  • TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) - Comprehensive guidance and practice for the TOEFL exam.
  • GRE (Graduate Record Examination) - Support for students preparing for GRE for graduate programs.
  • GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test) - Coaching and resources for GMAT preparation for MBA aspirants.
  • SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test) - Guidance and resources for high school students aiming for undergraduate studies.
