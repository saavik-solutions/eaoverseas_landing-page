Complaints Procedure

If you have a complaint about any aspect of our services, we encourage you to let us know. Your feedback is important to us and helps us to improve our services.

Step 1: Informal Resolution

Please contact us directly via email or phone to discuss your concerns. We aim to resolve issues quickly and informally.

Step 2: Formal Complaint

If you are not satisfied with the response to your informal complaint, you can submit a formal complaint. Please provide:

  • Your name and contact information
  • A detailed description of your complaint
  • Any relevant supporting documents

Send your formal complaint to: complaints@eaoverseas.com

Step 3: Acknowledgment

We will acknowledge receipt of your formal complaint within 5 working days and provide you with a timeline for the resolution.

Step 4: Investigation

We will investigate your complaint thoroughly and fairly. This process may take up to 20 working days. We will keep you informed of our progress.

Step 5: Resolution

Once the investigation is complete, we will provide you with our findings and any actions we plan to take. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may escalate the matter to an appropriate regulatory body.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding the complaints procedure, please contact us at: info@eaoverseas.com
